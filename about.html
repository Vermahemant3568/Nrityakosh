About Nrityakosh Academy

Nrityakosh Academy of Performing Arts
Founded by Nikitha Mani

Nikitha Mani, an accomplished Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam, and Kuchipudi dancer, began her training at the age of 5 under the guidance of eminent gurus Smt. Shanthi Krishna and Smt. Sreedevi Sujith. She has successfully completed her Mohiniyattam and Bharatanatyam Arangettams in 2012 and 2014, respectively.

Through Nrityakosh Academy of Performing Arts in Bangalore, Nikitha imparts classical dance training to students of all ages.
